## Brand & Style
The brand personality of the design system is rooted in **calm, clarity, and control**. It is designed for individuals seeking a distraction-free, fluid task management experience (ZenTodo). The visual language balances a high-tech modern feel with warm, soothing, and glassmorphic aesthetics.

The style uses expansive whitespace to reduce cognitive load and premium typographic treatments. Subtle depth is introduced through soft shadows, glassmorphism, and layered surfaces, creating a tactile sense of reliability.

## Colors
The palette is dominated by a vibrant **Primary Violet/Purple**, representing focus and mindfulness. A **Secondary Pink** provides visual interest, and a **Success Green** indicates completed tasks. The background is a sophisticated deep obsidian blue (#0B0F19).

## Typography
Outfit provides a clean, modern headline treatment. Inter provides legible, clear body text.

## Shapes
Soft rounded corners (16px/1rem radius) are used to create an organic, friendly, and premium feel.
